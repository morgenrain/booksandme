Please read each class introduction carefully, then download the 2018 FALL Class Registration Form. Fill out the information and bring the form and your check (made payable to Zhaoyu Cultural Foundation', to our Library to register for classes. If you sign up any Fall classes with 10 or more meetings, you can get $20 off from the tuition. Please contact our librarians for details.

Please Note:

*All classes are open only to our members, and require parent participation or present in the library.

* Most classes are taught in Mandarin, but all teachers are bilingual.

*Class sizes are small and popular classes filled out soon. Our policy is first come first serve, so please register now to get your spot!

*All classes, except Music Circle Time, do not allow parents/guardians inside the classrooms in order to give students a chance to learn independently. Parents and guardians must wait in the reading room/the library till the class ends.
