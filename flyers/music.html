2019 Summer Mandarin Circle Time

A new class combines singing, instrument, rhymes, games and Chinese-learning!

Designed for preschoolers aged 18 to 42 months who are building their vocabularies. Every week, one theme is introduced with frequently used Mandarin words and terms. The theme is reinforced with classic children’s Chinese songs, and coupled with percussion instruments, dances, rhymes, music games, and classical music appreciation to improve their focus and listening skills. A perfect first time experience for Mandarin immersion.

Saturdays/ 10:30-11:15 am (MCT6)

Session 1: 6/1, 6/8, 6/15, 6/22, 6/29, 7/13, 7/20 (7 weeks)

Small class size: Max. 10 /min. 5 students. In-class Parent PARTICIPATION REQUIRED.

Tuition: $161/kid for each 7-week session.

About Our Teachers

Ms. Jenny Huang has an MBA degree from Georgia State University and was a Business Analyst. She switched her focus to family after having two lovely kids. Teacher Huang has been hosting the Wednesday Baby/Toddler Story time since the establishment of Books and Me, and helped to create this Mandarin Music Circle Time. She is also a teacher at Palo Alto Chinese School.

Ms. Tiffany Wong has worked more than 10 years in Bay Area’s young children field. She was a preschool teacher at Appleseed Montessori School, and Director of Planning at New World Learning Center Bilingual Preschool, as well as School Director of Champion Chinese School. Teacher Wong has a master degree of Counselor Education from San Jose State University and runs her own family childcare center in Cupertino. Ms. Wong is famous for her energetic and active teaching style and always fun-filled class environment.
